About Us

We are an interdisciplinary research and advocacy initiative focused on supporting the implementation of the California Racial Justice Act (Cal. Penal § 745, RJA).

Our work centers on providing rigorous, evidence-based support to public defenders and legal teams advancing RJA claims. We bring together expertise we bring together expertise and social science to identify, analyze, and document racial bias in legal processes.

Our goal is simple: help ensure that the promise of the RJA is realized in practice.

About Us

We are an interdisciplinary research and advocacy initiative focused on supporting the implementation of the California Racial Justice Act (Cal. Penal § 745, RJA).

Our work centers on providing rigorous, evidence-based support to public defenders and legal teams advancing RJA claims. We bring together expertise in law and social science to identify, analyze, and document racial bias in legal processes.

Our goal is simple: help ensure that the promise of the RJA is realized in practice.

Our Objectives

Support RJA Litigation

Provide expert testimony, research, and analysis to strengthen individual RJA cases. Help build long-term infrastructure for supporting RJA claims across jurisdictions.

Produce Statistical Evidence

Conduct rigorous quantitative analyses that identify and document racial disparities in legal outcomes.

Build Accessible Knowledge

Develop accessible summaries of social science literature for use by public defenders and legal teams.

Translate Research into Practice

Translate primary and secondary research findings into actionable policy recommendations for legislators and practitioners.
